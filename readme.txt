== Description ==

= Show social proof ratings from various channels =
Proofratings monitors all your third party sites for reviews by your customers. Sharing review ratings badges on your website increases conversions. Proofratings focuses on solar and roofing companies to make them number one in their market.

All your reviews are collected to one dashboard, where you can decide which of them to feature in widgets.
Easily add these beautiful widgets to your website:
1. Floating widget
2. Embedded trust Badge widget
3. Banner Widget

= Showcase ratings on your website in real-time to build trust =
Increase your website's conversion by showing the ratings badges in the floating widget or embedded badges on your most important pages.

== Installation ==

1. Download and unzip the plugin
1. Upload the plugin folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
1. Go to Proofratings Dashboard to get started
